# Code Of Conduct

Roca is an open-source local-first AI project. We want the community to be
practical, respectful, and careful with user privacy.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and tradeoffs without personal attacks.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on reproducible behavior and user
  value.
- Avoid posting private prompts, transcripts, credentials, logs, or personal
  data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Doxxing or publishing private information without consent.
- Discrimination or exclusionary comments based on identity or background.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private transcripts, or exploit details in
  public project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Maintainers may also temporarily or
permanently limit participation when needed to protect the project and its
community.

For sensitive conduct concerns, do not open a public issue. Contact a
maintainer privately when possible, or use GitHub's abuse reporting tools for
urgent platform-level problems.
